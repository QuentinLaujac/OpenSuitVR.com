What is Open Suit VR ?

OpenSuitVR has been designed to immerse yourself in a virtual world

flash_on

Power Full

Over 25 motors are arranged on the combination and different sensors : pulse, temperature, position and breathing.



airplay

High compatibility

Developers can easily make OpenSuitVR compatible with their unity 3D games. Thanks to bluetooth technology, this suit can be used with all kind of smartphones in order to get the best mobile virtual reality experience.

group

User friendly

OpenSuitVR doesn't need any installation. It's super easy to use and ergonomic for people from 9 to 99 years old.




OpenSuitVR is not only about virtual reality ...

  • Never fall asleep

    Many people drive several hours a day, why not wake them up by a small vibration when it is detected they fall asleep?

  • Track sports activities

    We know smartwatch help people doing their sport but why not help them by adding other sensors and new kind of applications that can't be inserted in a small watch ?

  • Bad posture

    We never realize soon enough that our posture is bad, why not let OpenSuitVR warn us?
